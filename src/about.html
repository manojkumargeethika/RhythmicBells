About

Who we are

Rhythmic Bells is an online dance program that strives to ensure people of all ages and all backgrounds can have easy access to Bharatanatyam dance tutorials. These tutorials cover some of the most fundamental footwork and hand gestures used in the Indian classical dance form Bharatanatyam. Young beginner students can use this site as a way to get a head start on their dance career, and experienced students can use this to recollect the basics.
