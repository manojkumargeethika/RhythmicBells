Adavus (Footwork)

The fundamental footwork that is often seen in bharatanatyam dance routines are known as “adavus”. These steps are broken up into different categories. The two most basic categories of steps are called the Tatta Adavus and the Natta Adavus. These two categories have a total of 8 different steps each.
