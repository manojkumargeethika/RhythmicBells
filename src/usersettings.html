About

Bharatanatyam is considered one of the oldest forms of Indian classical dance. Bharatanatyam is graceful yet geometric, and has some of its own rules and methods that make it unique from other dance styles, from posture, to footwork, to hand movements.

This Site

This site is designed so that people of all ages and all learning capacities can learn or improve their skills in this dance style. With thorough explanation of the meaning and execution of the hand gestures and footwork in these video tutorials, dancers can see immense improvement in their skills. There are beginner, intermediate, and advanced categories depending on how much experience the student already has. Beginner tutorials show the basic hand gestures and footwork, while advanced level tutorials go into more complex hand and footwork. People could even start learning entire dance routines!
